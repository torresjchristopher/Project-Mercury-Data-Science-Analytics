## Domain Quirks & Characteristics

### Unique Challenges
1. **Real-Time vs Batch Tradeoffs** - Must balance latency, throughput, and consistency
2. **Data Drift Detection** - Models decay over time; need automated monitoring
3. **Feature Leakage Prevention** - Easy to accidentally use future data in training
4. **Distributed System Complexity** - Multi-machine coordination at scale
5. **Privacy at Scale** - How to train on sensitive data safely

### Technology Synergies
- Task 1 (Kafka) feeds into Task 5 (Analytics) for real-time insights
- Task 6 (Feature Store) is consumed by Task 2 (ML Observability)
- Task 7 (Privacy) enhances all other tasks' security posture
- Task 8 (Spark) processes raw data for Task 6 materialization
- Together = end-to-end ML infrastructure companies actually build
